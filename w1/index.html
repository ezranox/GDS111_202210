My Bio

I am not going to lie; writing a two paragraph bio about myself is hard, especially when I am trying to escape everything to do with me. I grew up in NYC, Queens; for 22 years of my life. I was always someone who stuck to themselves and it was hard for me to make friends up until Highschool. In highschool I became well known because of the group I hung out with, but they weren't the best people to have surrounded myself with. After fracturing my knee, I completely gave up on succeding in highschool. My dream of becoming a sports player or a Marine like my father was completely thrown off. For me, life grew worse and worse after that.. I lost track of everything that was important. My grades started depleting and I started to become someone that nobody knew. It was a suprise to me that I managed to graduate on time with the corect class. I proceeded to go straight to college in NYC. I thought that Law would be my best option, seeing that my mother was so excited for me to become a lawyer. With all of the pressure of being the first in my family to graduate I did my best to not fail my family. I wasn't happy at all..In what was suppoussed to be my junior year I met this girl just as COVID-19 was starting.. She told me that her mother was abusive and that she needed help. My family moved her into my home. We then got into a relationship where behind closed doors was nothing but traumatizing.. She became mentally, verbally, physically absuive..Amongst other things.. I lost hope once again within myself. I was trapped.
